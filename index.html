Provident Fund (EPF)

Overview

The Employees' Provident Fund and Miscellaneous Provisions Act, 1952 provides for the institution of provident funds, pension funds and deposit-linked insurance fund for employees.

Key Highlights:
  • Applicable to establishments with 20+ employees.
  • Employer contribution: 12% of Basic + DA.
  • Employee contribution: 12% of Basic + DA.

Employee State Insurance (ESIC)

Overview

The ESI Act provides for certain benefits to employees in case of sickness, maternity and employment injury.

Eligibility:

Applicable to non-seasonal factories using power and employing 10+ workers (wage limit: ₹21,000).

Note: Employer pays 3.25%, Employee pays 0.75%.

Payment of Gratuity Act

Overview

This Act provides for a scheme for the payment of gratuity to employees engaged in factories, mines, oilfields, plantations, ports, railway companies, shops or other establishments.

Calculation Formula:

Last Drawn Salary × 15/26 × Completed Years of Service

Minimum eligibility: 5 years of continuous service.

Shops & Establishment Act

Overview

Regulates the conditions of work in commercial establishments (Shops, Hotels, Restaurants, Theatres, etc.).

Key Provisions:
  • Working Hours (usually 9 hours/day).
  • Weekly Holidays.
  • Leave Entitlement (Casual, Sick, Earned).
  • Registration of the establishment.

BOCW Act (Building & Other Construction Workers)

Overview

An Act to provide for the safety, health and welfare measures and development and regeneration of the Building and Other Construction Workers.

Applicability:

Applicable to every establishment employing 10 or more workers on any day in the preceding 12 months.

Warning: Strict penalties apply for non-registration.
